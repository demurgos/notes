## List of words

The names are provided in camelCase.

- **canceled**
  - Not _cancelled_
- **indices**
  - Not _indexes_
  - _indices_ is used by Jetbrains
  - _indexes_ is used by Microsoft
- **pi**
  - In PascalCase, use **Pi**, not _PI_ (this is a normal word)
- **writable**
  - Not _writeable_
